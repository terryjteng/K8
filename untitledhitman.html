What is Untitled Open World Hitman?

Untitled Open World Hitman is an immersive stealth-action sandbox that combines the methodical planning of traditional stealth games with the creative freedom of an open-world environment. Drawing inspiration from the Hitman series while expanding into uncharted territory.

Every location becomes a puzzle with multiple solutions. Utilize environmental storytelling, dynamic AI systems, and emergent gameplay to craft unique assassination scenarios. Whether through social stealth, environmental manipulation, or direct action, the choice of approach is entirely yours.

Design Pillars

Emergent Creativity

Every environment is a playground for player creativity. Multiple routes, tools, and approaches ensure no two playthroughs are identical. The game rewards clever thinking and unconventional solutions.

Living, Reactive World

NPCs follow realistic schedules and routines. The world responds dynamically to player actions, creating opportunities for emergent storytelling and systemic gameplay interactions.

Precision Meets Freedom

Combine the methodical planning of classic stealth games with the exploration and discovery of open-world design. Each mission area offers depth and replayability.

Core Features

Sandbox Assassination

Large, open environments with multiple entry points, routes, and methods. Every mission location is a complex ecosystem waiting to be explored and exploited.

Dynamic AI Systems

Intelligent NPCs with realistic behavioral patterns, communication systems, and adaptive responses to player actions and environmental changes.

Environmental Storytelling

Rich narrative details woven into the world itself. Discover story fragments through exploration, observation, and interaction with the living environment.

Tools & Gadgets

A diverse arsenal of stealth tools, environmental manipulation devices, and creative problem-solving equipment that enable unique approach strategies.

Social Stealth

Blend into crowds, adopt disguises, and manipulate social situations. Use conversation, misdirection, and timing to achieve objectives without violence.

Consequence System

Actions have lasting effects on the world and story. Your methods and choices influence how characters, factions, and environments respond to your presence.
