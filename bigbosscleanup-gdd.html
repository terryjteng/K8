Core Gameplay Loop

Contract ‚Üí Prep ‚Üí Clean ‚Üí Repair ‚Üí Inspect ‚Üí Grade ‚Üí Payout
  • Contracts: pick arena, bonus objectives, penalties (time / chemical use / damage).
  • Prep: cordon area, set waste bins, choose loadout.
  • Cleaning: pressure wash, mop/chemical, vacuum shards, scrape decals & gum.
  • Repair: fix cracked tiles, bent rails, broken signage.
  • Inspect: UV/forensic light; grime % by type; hazard compliance.
  • Grade: Bronze‚ÄìS rank by time, completeness, waste sorting, damage.

  • Pressure Washer (nozzles; foam additive)
  • Mop + Bucket (detergent variants)
  • Vacuum (glass/metal shards)
  • Scraper (decals/gum), Rag (detail pass)

  • Paint FX splashes, soot/scorch, oil, ‚Äúmystery goop‚Äù (non-bio)
  • Physics debris: glass, tiles, props
