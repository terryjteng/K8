Logline

What is Corebound?

Corebound is a narrative-driven 2D side-scroller blending the fast-paced action of Mega Man X with the atmospheric exploration and emotional depth of Hollow Knight. Players experience a retro-style adventure with real-time combat, deep exploration, and branching storylines where every choice carries weight.

Wake up in the desert with no memory. See a dystopian city in the distance. To survive the journey and uncover the truth, you must make a harrowing choice: trade parts of your human body for mechanical upgrades. Each enhancement grants new abilities but strips away your humanity, piece by piece. The game explores the cost of survival and what it means to lose yourself to become something moreâ€”or lessâ€”than human.
