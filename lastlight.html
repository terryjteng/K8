2D Zombie Survival & Home-Fortification Game

Last Light

A 2D zombie survival and home-fortification game built around one idea: your home is everything. Gather wood, stone, and metal, reinforce your home, build turrets, and manage a shared ammo pool between yourself and your defenses.

Full Game Vision View Demo Scope

Overview

What is Last Light?

Last Light is a 2D zombie survival and home-fortification game. Players gather wood, stone, and metal, return to their home, and use those resources to strengthen the structure, build turrets, and prepare for future zombie threats while managing a shared ammo pool used by both the player and their turrets.

Design Pillars

Home at the Center

The home is the heart of the experience. Resource choices and upgrades revolve around protecting and strengthening this one space.

Shared Ammo Decisions

The same ammo supports both you and your turrets. Every shot you take is one less round your defenses can use later.

Simple Inputs, Clear Goals

Core actions stay straightforward: gather, fortify, and survive. The depth comes from how you choose to invest limited resources into your home and defenses.

Core Elements

Resource Gathering

Collect wood, stone, and metal. These materials are the backbone of your home upgrades and turret construction.

Home Upgrades

Reinforce and improve your home using gathered resources. The home’s condition and layout determine how well you can withstand zombie threats.

Turrets

Build turrets that draw from the same ammo pool you use. Turrets extend your defense but also consume precious ammunition.

Machete & Gun

Swap between close-range melee with the machete and ranged use of the gun, always aware that gunfire draws from the shared ammo pool.

Zombie Threats

Zombies are the constant pressure on your home. How you prepare with resources, upgrades, and ammo management shapes how long you can hold out.

Demo → Full Game

The demo focuses on proving this loop with a contained scope. The full game expands on these same systems rather than reinventing them.

Play Pattern

Gather • Fortify • Survive

  • Gather: Obtain wood, stone, and metal for your home.
  • Fortify: Spend resources to upgrade and strengthen the home and build turrets.
  • Prepare: Manage how much ammo is available for both you and your turrets.
  • Survive: Use your machete, gun, and turrets together to keep the home standing against zombies.
Key Tension
How you spend resources and ammo to keep the home safe.
Demo Purpose
Test and prove this loop on a small, clearly defined scope.
Full Game
Extend the loop with more ways to fortify and respond to zombie threats.

Development Path

From Demo to Full Game

Last Light is structured so the demo and full game share the same foundation: gathering resources, fortifying a home, using turrets, and managing shared ammo.

Phase 1: Demo

Implement the core elements: resources, home upgrades, turrets, machete, gun, and shared ammo.

Phase 2: Expanded Scope

Build on top of the demo by adding more ways to use wood, stone, and metal, and more possibilities for how players fortify their home and use turrets.

Phase 3: Full Game Polish

Refine pacing, presentation, and quality-of-life based on feedback, keeping the core focus on survival and home-fortification with shared ammo.
