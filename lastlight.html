Zombie Defense Farm Sim

Last Light

A cozy-meets-chaos zombie defense where the goal is simple and ambitious: build the ultimate home base. Farm and craft by day, fortify and defend by night. A charming 2D pixel art survival sim that feels like Harvest Moon meets Stardew Valley with the tension of Project Zomboid.

Logline

What is Last Light?

A charming 2D survival sim about turning a derelict plot into a fortified haven. Grow food, craft defenses, recruit neighbors, and design ingenious base layouts to withstand escalating nightly raids. Your layout—and your choices—write the story.

Design Pillars

Base First

The base is the hero. Layout, choke points, traps, power routing, and upgrade paths are the core puzzle.

Cozy Day / Tense Night

Meditative farming and building by day; tactical defense and panic-management by night.

Neighbors & Stories

Rescue and recruit NPCs with unique perks; their survival unlocks new stations, tech, and narrative beats.

Core Features

Deep Base Building

Walls, gates, platforms; trap types (spike, flame, knockback), power grids, auto-turrets, sensor logic.

Farming & Crafting

Crops and preserves; metal/wood/stone chains; workbenches, generators, water collection, greenhouse upgrades.

Raids & AI

Pathfinding waves that adapt to your layout; boss variants; weather and blackout events that change tactics.

Companions

Recruit specialists (Engineer, Farmer, Guard). Assign shifts, morale buffs, and emergency protocols.

Progression

Tech tree from makeshift to industrial; blueprint hunts; prestige “New Threat” modes with modifiers.

Shareable Bases

Photo mode + blueprint export; creator challenges (limited resources, permadeath, storm season).

Future Multiplayer Potential

Last Light is designed with multiplayer expansion in mind. Post-launch, we plan to explore cooperative defense modes where players can join forces to defend shared bases, compete in timed challenges, or trade resources in a persistent world. The core systems are built to support online integration with minimal refactoring.

Loop

Farm • Build • Defend • Evolve

  • Morning: Harvest, craft, plan upgrades, assign NPC tasks.
  • Afternoon: Expand walls, lay traps, scout for parts, rescue survivors.
  • Night: Defend the base—repair, reroute power, hold the line.
  • After Action: Analyze damage heatmaps, unlock new tech, redesign chokepoints.

Production

Team

Producer/Designer, Unity Developer, 2D Pixel Artist, 2D Animator, Sound Composer; rev-share QA.

Tech

Unity 2D, tile-based building grid, pathfinding AI, damage heatmaps, save states, blueprint export system.

Risks & Mitigation

Scope → gated tech tree & episodic seasons; Balance → telemetry + weekly playtests; Pixel art pipeline → modular sprite system.

Development Roadmap

Concept to Playable

Last Light is in early concept phase. Our roadmap focuses on rapid prototyping and validation.

Phase 1: Concept & Prototyping (8-10 weeks)

Build core loop: grid-based building, basic resource gathering, simple raid encounter. Validate "tower defense meets base builder" hook.

Phase 2: Vertical Slice (12-14 weeks)

Expand to 2-3 biomes, tech tree progression, power grid system, environmental storytelling. Create investor-ready demo build.

Phase 3: Pre-Production (16-20 weeks)

Secure funding, finalize scope, build production team. Create full GDD, technical architecture, art bible. Plan for 18-month production cycle.
