3D Cleanup Simulator

Clean Up After the Fight

After the match ends, someone has to clean the arena. Big Boss Cleanup is a 3D first-person cleanup simulator where you mop puddles, sweep shards, and collect debris after chaotic fighting matches. Complete jobs to earn money for better equipment upgrades, and take on timed challenges or clean up during active fights for bonus pay.

Overview

What is Big Boss Cleanup?

Big Boss Cleanup is a 3D first-person cleanup simulator where you step into the aftermath of intense arena battles. Navigate the arena after each brawl, using professional cleaning tools to mop puddles, sweep glass shards, and clear debris before the next match begins.

Similar to other simulator games, completing jobs earns you money to purchase better equipment and upgrades. The more efficiently you clean, the more you earn. Take on optional challenges for bonus pay—race against the clock before the next fight starts, or brave cleaning during an active match while dodging fighters and avoiding new messes being created in real-time.

Core Mechanics

Gameplay

Arena Cleanup

Navigate arenas in first-person view, using your mop and broom to clean up after intense battles. Each job completed earns money for upgrades.

Equipment Progression

Earn money from completed jobs to purchase better cleaning tools and equipment. Upgrade your arsenal to handle tougher messes more efficiently.

Timed Challenges

Clean up before the next fight begins. Race against the clock to complete the job and earn bonus pay for speed and thoroughness.

Active Fight Mode

Take on high-risk, high-reward jobs cleaning during live matches. Avoid fighters, dodge obstacles, and manage new messes being created in real-time.

Physics-Based Cleaning

Realistic 3D physics-driven interactions. Mop puddles, sweep shards, and move debris with satisfying, tactile feedback.

Challenge System

Optional modifiers increase difficulty and payout. Balance risk versus reward as you decide which challenges to accept.

Design Pillars

Immersive Simulation

First-person perspective with realistic physics creates an engaging, tactile cleaning experience. Feel the satisfaction of a job well done.

Risk & Reward

Choose your challenges wisely. Timed cleanups and active fight mode offer higher payouts but demand quick thinking and precise execution.

Progression System

Earn money from jobs to upgrade your equipment. Build your cleaning business from basic tools to professional-grade gear.

Development Roadmap

High-Level Milestones

Phase 1: Pre-Production (4 weeks)

Finalize parody-safe creative vision and gameplay pillars; define technical scope and system foundations.

Phase 2: Prototype (10–12 weeks)

Build a playable arena with functional cleaning mechanics and grime systems.

Phase 3: Vertical Slice (16–18 weeks)

Expand prototype into an investor-ready demo with full gameplay loop, UI, and grading.

Phase 4: Full Production M1 (6 months)

Implement progression systems, contracts, and advanced grime logic.

Phase 5: Full Production M2 (6 months)

Expand content library and refine game feel for Alpha.

Phase 6: Alpha / Playtest (2–3 months)

Closed playtests and feature lock.

Phase 7: Beta / Marketing Prep (3 months)

Final polish, Steam demo, trailer, PR.

Phase 8: Launch + Post-Launch (TBD)

Release, live ops, mod support.
