Last Light

2D Zombie Defense Farm Sim • GDD + Pitch

A charming 2D pixel art survival game where cozy farming by day meets tense base defense by night. Build the ultimate homestead against the undead.

Studio: K.8
Build Path: Prototype → Vertical Slice → Partner Build
Contact: terryjteng@gmail.com

Executive Summary

  • Core: A 2D top-down farming and base-building survival sim where you craft, fortify, and defend against nightly zombie hordes.
  • Market Fit: Fans of Stardew Valley, Harvest Moon, and Project Zomboid—cozy farm sim meets tactical defense.
  • Art Style: Charming pixel art with detailed environmental storytelling and expressive character animations.
  • Unique Hook: Day/night rhythm where your base layout and defensive strategy are just as important as farming efficiency.
  • Prototype (12w): Core building grid, crops, basic raids, traps v1, repair loop, simple survivor.
  • VS (+16w): Three biomes, tech tree, power/sensors, boss raids, blueprint export, playtests.

Design Pillars

Base First

The homestead is the hero. Layout decisions, choke points, trap placement, and upgrade paths form the core strategic puzzle.

Cozy Day / Tense Night

Meditative farming and crafting by day transforms into tactical defense and panic-management when the sun sets.

Neighbors & Stories

Rescue and recruit NPCs with unique abilities and backstories. Their survival unlocks new crafting stations, tech, and narrative moments.

Core Loop: Farm • Build • Defend • Evolve

Morning Phase
  • Harvest crops and collect resources
  • Craft materials at workbenches
  • Plan base expansions and upgrades
  • Assign NPC tasks and schedules
Afternoon Phase
  • Build walls, gates, and platforms
  • Lay traps and set up defenses
  • Scout for salvage and parts
  • Rescue survivors from nearby areas
Night Phase (Defense)
  • Defend against zombie waves
  • Repair damaged structures in real-time
  • Reroute power to critical systems
  • Hold defensive positions with NPCs
After Action
  • Review damage heatmaps and breach points
  • Unlock new tech from research bench
  • Redesign chokepoints and trap layouts
  • Plan next day's improvements

Farming & Crafting Systems

Crops & Food Production
  • 10+ crop types with varying growth rates
  • Seasonal variants and weather effects
  • Food preservation: drying, canning, smoking
  • Greenhouse upgrades for year-round farming
  • Water collection and irrigation systems
Resource Chains
  • Wood → Planks → Reinforced Walls
  • Metal Scrap → Plates → Turrets/Traps
  • Stone → Brick → Fortified Foundations
  • Electronics → Circuits → Power Grid
Crafting Stations
  • Workbench: Basic tools and repairs
  • Forge: Metal items and weapons
  • Electronics Bench: Sensors and power
  • Chemistry Station: Medicine and fuel
  • Research Desk: Tech tree unlocks
Blueprint System
  • Discover blueprints through exploration
  • Learn from rescued NPCs
  • Research advanced tech combinations
  • Export/share base designs with community

Base Building & Defense

Structural Elements
  • Walls (Wood → Stone → Reinforced Metal)
  • Gates and doors with lock systems
  • Elevated platforms and watchtowers
  • Underground bunkers and storage
  • Modular room system for specialization
Trap Types
  • Spike Traps: Ground-level damage zones
  • Flame Traps: Area denial and crowd control
  • Knockback Pads: Launch zombies off cliffs
  • Electric Fences: Stun and damage combo
  • Explosive Barrels: Manual detonation timing
Power Grid System
  • Generators (Gas, Solar, Wind)
  • Power routing with cable management
  • Battery banks for backup power
  • Priority systems during brownouts
  • Auto-turrets and sensor networks
Advanced Defense
  • Auto-Turrets: Ammo-based automated guns
  • Sensor Logic: Trigger traps on detection
  • Spotlight System: Reveal zombies, boost accuracy
  • Loudspeakers: Lure zombies to kill zones
  • Emergency Protocols: NPC fallback positions

NPC Companions & Recruitment

Engineer

Abilities: Repair structures 50% faster, unlock automated systems, reduce power consumption.

Story: Former electrician searching for family.

Farmer

Abilities: Crops grow 30% faster, improved harvest yields, can tend animals.

Story: Homesteader who lost everything to the horde.

Guard

Abilities: Improved combat damage, can man turrets, boosts nearby NPC morale.

Story: Former military trying to protect civilians.

NPC Management
  • Assign day/night shifts and priorities
  • Morale system affects work efficiency
  • Permanent death if not rescued during breaches
  • Relationship building unlocks personal quests
  • Each survivor brings unique tech/recipes

Enemy AI & Raid System

Zombie Types
  • Shambler: Slow, low health, swarm tactics
  • Runner: Fast, flanking behavior, priority target
  • Brute: High HP, breaks walls, siege unit
  • Spitter: Ranged acid attacks, stays back
  • Screamer: Calls reinforcements, support unit
Pathfinding AI
  • Dynamic path calculation around walls
  • Exploits weak points in defenses
  • Avoids kill zones after repeated failures
  • Coordinated multi-direction assaults
Raid Progression
  • Wave 1-5: Basic shamblers, learn patterns
  • Wave 6-10: Mixed groups, runners appear
  • Wave 11-15: Brutes and spitters join
  • Wave 16+: Boss variants and special events
Special Events
  • Blood Moon: 2x spawn rate, boss guaranteed
  • Blackout Storm: Power fails, limited vision
  • Fog Night: Reduced sensor range
  • Migration: Mega-horde from one direction
  • Quiet Night: No raid, perfect for repairs

Technical Architecture

Engine & Tools
  • Engine: Unity 2D (LTS version)
  • Version Control: Git with LFS for assets
  • Art Pipeline: Aseprite for pixel art
  • Audio: FMOD or Unity Audio Mixer
  • Localization: Unity Localization package
Core Systems (ScriptableObjects)
  • `CropSO { id, growthTime, seasons[], yield }`
  • `StructureSO { id, health, tier, resources[] }`
  • `TrapSO { id, damage, range, cooldown }`
  • `ZombieSO { id, speed, hp, ai_behavior }`
  • `NPCSO { id, skills[], morale, backstory }`
Save System
  • JSON-based world state serialization
  • Incremental saves every dawn phase
  • Cloud save sync (Steam, console)
  • Backup system for corruption recovery
  • Blueprint export as separate files
Performance Targets
  • PC: 60 FPS on mid-tier hardware
  • Console: Stable 30 FPS on last-gen
  • Max Entities: 200+ zombies on screen
  • Build Size: Under 500MB
  • Load Times: Under 10 seconds

Risk Analysis & Mitigation

Scope Creep

Risk: Feature additions delay launch.

Mitigation:

  • Gated tech tree allows episodic content
  • Clear milestone acceptance criteria
  • MVP feature set locked after prototype
  • Post-launch DLC for seasonal content

Balance Issues

Risk: Defense too easy/hard, ruins fun.

Mitigation:

  • Weekly internal playtests
  • Telemetry on survival rates and trap usage
  • Public alpha for balance feedback
  • Difficulty settings for accessibility

Art Pipeline

Risk: Pixel art production bottleneck.

Mitigation:

  • Modular sprite system for reuse
  • Asset library from early prototyping
  • Clear style guide and templates
  • Burst hiring for asset sprints
