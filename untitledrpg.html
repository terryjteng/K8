RPG Concept · Early Development

Untitled RPG (Working Title)

A turn-based RPG concept inspired by Chrono Trigger, EarthBound, and Super Mario RPG, with a narrative approach influenced by the choice-driven storytelling of Baldur's Gate 3.

Contact About This Project

Premise

World & Story Setup

An alien ship crash-lands on Earth and triggers a portal that links a medieval world with a future setting. After a close friend disappears while interacting with the crash site, the player character is pulled into a cross-era journey to find them and understand why the worlds have collided.

  • Alien crash as the inciting incident that connects eras.
  • Portal linking medieval and sci-fi futures into a single, unstable reality.
  • A missing friend at the heart of the main quest.
  • Decisions meant to influence how the journey unfolds and how people respond to you.

Gameplay Direction

Turn-Based Battles

Combat is planned as classic, menu-driven turn-based battles in the tradition of SNES-era JRPGs. The exact mechanics (skills, progression, and encounter pacing) are still in active design and not yet finalized.

Exploration & Map

Players move between locations connected across time, exploring a larger map inspired by games like Chrono Trigger and early Pokémon titles. Specific regions, dungeons, and side-areas are still being mapped out.

Player Choice

The goal is to support meaningful decisions—who you help, how you respond, and where you go—that influence scenes, quest outcomes, and character reactions. The exact branching structure and number of endings are intentionally not locked yet.

Narrative Focus

Key Themes

The story centers on connection across distance and time, the cost of curiosity, and how different cultures handle a shared crisis. The portal forces medieval and futuristic societies to confront each other, with the player caught in the middle while trying to bring their friend home.

Planned Pillars

  • A personal, character-driven main quest anchored on the missing friend.
  • Conflicting perspectives between eras (tradition vs. progress, faith vs. science).
  • Moments where player choices affect relationships and the tone of key scenes.
  • Room for humor, warmth, and grounded interactions despite the sci-fi premise.

Development

Where It Is Now

Untitled RPG is currently in a concept and planning phase. The focus right now is on:

  • Refining the core premise and major story arcs.
  • Defining the turn-based combat feel at a high level.
  • Outlining the structure of the world map and key locations.
  • Identifying what kinds of choices should have visible consequences.

What’s Still TBD

Many details are intentionally not fixed yet to keep the design flexible:

  • Specific combat systems (turn order logic, combo systems, etc.).
  • Platform targets and technical stack.
  • Team size, production schedule, and release window.
  • Exact scope of branching paths and endings.

This page is meant to reflect only confirmed, high-level intentions and will evolve as the design is finalized.
