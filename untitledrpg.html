Untitled RPG

A tribute to classic, turn-based JRPGs—think Chrono Trigger, EarthBound, and FFIII—reimagined with a modern narrative system and an expansive decision tree inspired by BG3 and Detroit.

Logline

What is Untitled RPG?

A party-based, turn-based adventure where time-bending choices ripple through character arcs, locations, and boss routes. Emphasis on snappy battles, combo abilities, and a grounded, heartfelt story that branches widely without losing coherence.

Genre
Turn-Based RPG • Party Adventure
Combat
Timeline & combo-driven; status synergies; limit abilities
Stage
Concept → Prototype
Platform
PC (primary), Console (secondary)
Session
20–40 min quest beats • Boss routes

Design Pillars

Classic, Snappy Turn-Based

Readable turn order, dual-tech style combos, and status synergies that reward planning without bogging down pacing.

Expansive Decision Tree

Branching quests and character loyalties; decisions meaningfully reroute scenes, companions, and boss ladders.

Time & Consequence

Light time-skips and state changes reflect what the player chose—towns evolve, NPCs remember, endings vary.

Core Features

Party Bonds

Dialogue choices shape duo/trio abilities and unlock unique combo arts in combat.

Route Variants

Multiple dungeon orders and optional boss lines; choices surface new paths and close others.

Overworld With Purpose

Exploration tightens narrative beats—town states, side quests, and crafting reflect your moral trajectory.

Modern UX

Fast travel, battle speed toggles, readable logs, quest tracking, and lossless respec windows.

Streamer-Friendly Modes

Seeded runs for challenge paths; spoiler-safe mode for first playthroughs.

Soundtrack First

Memorable leitmotifs for party members and regions; modern mix with retro instrumentation.

Production

Team

Producer/Designer, Unity Engineer, 2D/Pixel Artist, Narrative Designer, Composer; rev-share contributors for UI and additional art.

Tech

Unity 2D; timeline/ATB-like combat system; ScriptableObjects for skills, status, AI routines.

Risks & Mitigation

Branch sprawl → act-based gates; Content load → modular quest templates; Balancing → weekly playtest cadence.

Milestones

Prototype (Now → +14 weeks)

Combat core, 2 party members, 1 dungeon, 1 boss line, baseline decision tree.

Vertical Slice (+14 → +32 weeks)

3 dungeons, 4–5 party members, combo system, branching questline v1, art & audio pass, playtests.

Partner Build (+32 → +44 weeks)

Roadshow demo, wishlist campaign, creator program, platform submissions.
